\begin{abstract}
We study Ricci curvature properties of Hessian metrics on the leaves of the 
codimension-one foliation $\mathcal{F} = \ker\omega$ generated by the first 
Koszul form $\omega$ of a Hessian manifold $(M^d, g, \nabla)$. 
\end{abstract}
